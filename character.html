Dragon Ball Characters


Goku
Vegeta

Vegeta is the prince of the fallen Saiyan race.

Frieza

Frieza is one of the most feared villains in Dragon Ball.

Piccolo

Piccolo is a wise and powerful Namekian.

Gohan

Gohan is Goku's eldest son and a powerful warrior.

Trunks

Trunks is the son of Vegeta and Bulma, known for time traveling.

Krillin

Krillin is one of Goku's best friends and a brave warrior.

Bulma

Bulma is a scientific genius and close friend of Goku.

Cell

Cell is a bio-engineered villain and one of Goku's deadliest foes.

Majin Buu

Majin Buu is a powerful villain known for his regeneration ability.

Beerus

Beerus is the God of Destruction, feared across the universe.

Whis

Whis is the attendant of Beerus and one of the strongest beings.

Broly

Broly is the Legendary Super Saiyan, an unstoppable force.

Chi-Chi

Chi-Chi is Goku's wife and mother to Gohan and Goten.

Yamcha

Yamcha is a former desert bandit and a close ally of Goku.
