Sagas


The Hunt for the Dragon Balls

The journey begins with the Emperor Pilaf Saga, where a young Goku teams up with Bulma and other allies to search for the seven Dragon Balls. Along the way, they face challenges from Emperor Pilaf, who seeks the Dragon Balls for world domination. This introductory saga sets the tone for the series, introducing Goku's strength and the magic of the Dragon Balls.

Tournament and Red Ribbon Army

After Goku's initial adventures, the Tournament Saga sees him entering the World Martial Arts Tournament, where he faces strong fighters like Jackie Chun and learns valuable lessons. Following this is the Red Ribbon Army Saga, where Goku battles the powerful Red Ribbon Army to recover the Dragon Balls. The General Blue Saga and Commander Red Saga continue this storyline, leading to Goku's intense battle against the evil forces of the Red Ribbon Army.

The Dark Tournament and Demon King Piccolo

The stakes rise with the Tien Shinhan Saga, which features Goku entering another World Martial Arts Tournament, where he encounters the deadly Tien Shinhan. Afterward, the King Piccolo Saga introduces a major threat as King Piccolo, an ancient demon, seeks to conquer the world. Goku must find new strength to defeat him. The saga concludes with the Piccolo Jr. Saga, where King Piccolo's reincarnated son enters the tournament, setting up one of Goku's fiercest rivalries.

Enter the Saiyans

The start of Dragon Ball Z brings the Saiyan Saga, where Goku learns about his true heritage as a Saiyan warrior. His brother, Raditz, arrives on Earth, revealing that Goku was sent to conquer the planet. With the arrival of Vegeta and Nappa, the saga escalates into one of the most intense battles for Earth’s survival, with Goku pushing his limits to defend his friends.

The Battle on Namek

The Frieza Saga takes the adventure to space, where Goku, Vegeta, and their friends travel to Planet Namek to gather the Dragon Balls and stop the tyrant Frieza. Frieza’s power is unmatched, but Goku awakens the legendary Super Saiyan transformation, resulting in one of the most iconic battles in anime history. This saga cements Goku’s place as one of the universe's strongest fighters.

Androids and Cell

The Android Saga begins when a mysterious warning from the future reveals that powerful androids will soon threaten Earth. Goku and the Z Fighters must prepare to face the deadly Android 17 and 18, as well as Dr. Gero’s ultimate creation: Cell. The saga culminates in the Cell Games, where Gohan, Goku’s son, becomes the hero of the story by defeating Cell with his Super Saiyan 2 transformation.

The Rise of Majin Buu

The Majin Buu Saga introduces a magical and terrifying new enemy, Majin Buu. This saga is marked by intense battles, the return of fusion techniques, and the ultimate sacrifice by Goku to stop Buu’s destruction. As Goku attains new powers, including Super Saiyan 3, the fight for Earth’s survival becomes more desperate than ever. Ultimately, with the help of his friends, Goku defeats Buu using the Spirit Bomb.

Gods of Destruction and Resurrection

In Dragon Ball Super, the story expands with the Battle of Gods Saga, introducing Beerus, the God of Destruction, who challenges Goku to reach the power of a Super Saiyan God. The saga continues with the Golden Frieza Saga, where Frieza returns from the dead, stronger than ever, forcing Goku and Vegeta to unlock new forms to stop him.

Multiverse and Goku Black

The Universe 6 Saga brings a multiverse twist, where Goku and his team fight in a tournament against warriors from Universe 6, including Saiyans from another universe. The stakes escalate in the Goku Black Saga, where a future version of Goku turns evil and wreaks havoc across timelines. Goku and Vegeta must work with Future Trunks to stop Goku Black and the rogue Kai, Zamasu.

Tournament of Power

The Tournament of Power Saga pushes Goku and the Z Fighters to their limits, as multiple universes compete in a battle royale to avoid erasure. The saga introduces new powerful fighters, such as Jiren from Universe 11. Goku’s Ultra Instinct transformation is a highlight of the saga, as it allows him to fight on instinct, pushing beyond his previous limits.

Broly Returns

The Broly Saga (from the Dragon Ball Super: Broly movie) reintroduces the fan-favorite character Broly, but this time with a new backstory. Goku and Vegeta must face off against Broly, who possesses uncontrollable power. The saga adds depth to Saiyan history and showcases an epic battle between the three Saiyans.

Dragon Ball Heroes Saga

The Dragon Ball Heroes Saga is an anime promotion of the video game series, showcasing a mix of canonical and non-canonical characters. This saga features new enemies, time travel, and fusion techniques. It takes the story into new, fan-service-driven directions, allowing for battles that transcend universes and timelines.

Dragon Ball Daima

The newest chapter in the Dragon Ball universe, Dragon Ball Daima, brings back the youthful, adventurous spirit of the original series with a fresh twist. Set to release soon, this saga is expected to introduce new characters, challenges, and threats, with Goku and his friends facing an entirely new era of battles. With a return to Goku’s roots and the charm of earlier Dragon Ball, fans eagerly await the next great adventure in the series.
